About Us

25 Years of Financial
Excellence

Amirtharaj Investments has been a trusted name in mutual fund distribution since 1999, headquartered in the heart of Mylapore, Chennai. Our journey began with a single belief: that every Indian deserves access to world-class investment advisory — not just the privileged few.

Today, we serve a growing family of over 500 investors across India and abroad, managing portfolios with a disciplined, data-first philosophy. As an AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor and AMFI Registered SIF Distributor, every recommendation we make is compliant, transparent, and in the investor's best interest.
